My Vacation in Capetown

The Mother City

What was nice about my stay in Capetown?

CapeTown was an incredible destination! I loved the stunning natural beauty, friendly people, and vibrant culture. The hiking trails, local cruisine, and sunset views were highlights of my trip. It was great opportunity to relax and try new experiences. I'll always cherish the memories of my time in Capetown.

Places I enjoyed the most

Atlantis Dunes in Cape Town offers quad biking tours with photo opportunities. You can explore the 27 square kilometers of white sand dunes, sometimes reaching the height of a six-story building, while riding ATVs. Guides will snap photos of you and your group during the ride. Atlantis Dunes also offers separate photo shoot packages, including transport into the dunes.

The Bo-Kaap is one of Cape Town's most distinct neighbourhoods known for its vibrant, predominantly Muslim, Cape Malay community and its equally bold flat-roofed colourful houses and cobbled stone roads.
