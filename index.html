My Vacation in Capetown-The Mother City

Why Capetown?

Capetown is a modern city surrounded by nature.Centred around table mountain, one of the new 7 wonders of the world, making it one of the most beautiful cities in the world, and there are plenty reasons to visit.I had so much fun during my stay in Capetown, I enjoyed sight-seeing, from the white sandy bays, wine farms.The City has vibrant people too, I just love capetown!
